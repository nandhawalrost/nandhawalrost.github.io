About

Before running I was a karateka and futsal player (nyeker/barefoot), and a little bit of football (soccer as if you are american :p) school in my childhood and early teenage, with that background I'm feeling lucky I have good enough discipline and other fundamental factors before I'm into running. I started running because that is something I can sustain and a lot of freedom than my previous sport discipline, it is also fun to experiment with the sport science: you can turn your body into a race lab with careful and mindful consideration. For a tool of verification I took a certification and choosing Dr. Jack Daniels System (VDOT) because I'm already familiar with that before, I also learn from Marius Bakken, Dr. Jason Karp, Steve Magness (my running science mainly influenced by them).

In my romantic relation with distance running❤️ I do agree running makes you more and more confident, disciplined, dedicated, and happy. That's the pride and joy, that's approaching a perfect life mentally.
